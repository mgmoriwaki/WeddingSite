The Proposal

On April 2, 2017 Alex and I were making pancakes for breakfast. While I was mixing the batter Alex started to say, "So for lunch..." and I was thinking to myself, "Lunch, really? We're still in the process of making breakfast!" Then Alex finished his sentence, "...do you want to go on a picnic?" Keep in mind we had just gotten about 2.5 feet of snow the day before. Where in the world would he want to picnic? I suggested the living room but Alex had a different idea...

I thought he might be up to something so I tried to play it cool and agreed to the picnic-lunch-in-the-snow plan. That afternoon Alex packed up lunch and we hopped in the car. We ended up in Kensington at the town park. In high school we often went there after taking Buddy for a walk.

It was a beautiful sunny day, though we still had to tromp through the snow to the pavilion picnic area. We ate lunch in the sun and Alex ever so casually told me how much I mean to him, basically greasing my wheels to work his way up to the big question.

I was facing away from Alex noting how a tree near the pavilion had really weird banches when Alex said, "Hey Melis..." and I turned around to find him down on one knee holding something quite sparkly, "Will you marry me?" Of course I blurted out "YEAH! I mean YES!" I felt like I had to be more formal about it.

And now we're here excitingly planning our wedding for August 10, 2018!!!
